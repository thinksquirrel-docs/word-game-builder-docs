Fluid Collision Sound

Overview

The Fluid Collision Sound plugin plays a sound when it collides with a fluid. It can also play another high-impact sound.

Note
This plugin requires the Fluid Rigidbody Particles component to be present on the target fluid.
See Also
FluidCollisionSound

Properties

  • Plugin Type - Use this to change the plugin component type. You will need to reselect the game object after changing this property.
  • Fluid - The fluid associated with this plugin.
  • Collision Source - The audio source to play low-impact collision sounds from.
  • Collision Velocity - The minimum velocity required from a particle for a low-impact collision sound to play.
  • Min Collisions - The minimum amount of collisions needed, in succession, in order to play a low-impact collision sound.
  • Collision Fade Time - The fade time (in seconds) for the low-impact collision sound.
  • High Impact Source - The audio source to play high-impact collision sounds from.
  • Impact Velocity - The minimum velocity required from a particle for a high-impact collision sound to play.
  • Min High Impact Collisions - The minimum amount of collisions needed, in succession, in order to play a high-impact collision sound.
  • Scale Pitch With Time - If enabled, the pitch of the collision sounds are scaled with the current time scale.
